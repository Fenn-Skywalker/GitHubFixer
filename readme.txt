Objective:
Collect all keys in each level to unlock the door 
After the door is unlocked reach it to 

Controls:
Left arrow key - Move left
Right arrow key - Move right
Up arrow key - Jump

"admin" controls:
space - slows down time and enables level swapping 
down arrow key - loads the next level

Notes:
The player moves based on acceleration and velocity and 
will only automatically slow down when on the floor
and the player presses no keys, otherwise the player will 
"slide" in the direction of the last key pressed until his velocity 
reaches 0 so be mindful not to slip off edges!

Known Issues:
Player can experience some ghost collisions when jumping
Occasionally when landing the player may snap to corners
